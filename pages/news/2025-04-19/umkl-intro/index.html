Welcome to the UMKL!

19/04/2025 | Intro

Welcome to the University Mario Kart League!

If you've gotten this far, you probably know what we're about, but if not, we're glad to have you. We're an independent gaming league of university teams and society off our heads obsessed with competitive Mario Kart.

This passion project started last year, with only four university teams in play, but has quickly expanded throughout Midlands universities and beyond. We're ever expanding, with test 6v6 matches and friendlies tiding us over until our next season starts next academic year.
(Season 2 is scheduled to start around October 2025)

We're one season down, with our reigning champions York currently topping the tables. While we're still tweaking our website and our roster for next season, we are currently in a friendly fever, hosting practice matches often as we grow. Our discord is growing daily, with more and more interest being shown in our mission to grow university level competitive Mario kart. We are run by students, for students, and we remain dedicated to forging connections between universities and carving our own little niche in gaming culture.

Our 6v6 comp format gives uni groups ample opportunity to communicate with each other, improve their Mario Kart 8 Deluxe skills, and grow stronger together. With twelve matches and twelve players per match, there's ample opportunity to practice your greenshell snipes… or face the wrath of your teammates from friendly fire.
